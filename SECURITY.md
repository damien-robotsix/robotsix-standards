# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please report it
privately. **Do not open a public issue.**

### Contact

Email: [security@robotsix.dev](mailto:security@robotsix.dev)

### Response Time

We aim to acknowledge vulnerability reports within **5 business days** and
provide an initial assessment within **10 business days**.

### Coordinated Disclosure

We practice coordinated disclosure: we ask that you give us a reasonable
window to develop and deploy a fix before publicly discussing the
vulnerability. Once a fix is released we encourage public discussion and
credit the reporter (unless they prefer to remain anonymous).

### Scope

This policy covers the robotsix-standards documentation site and any
supporting tooling in this repository.

### Out of Scope

- Vulnerability reports about third-party services or dependencies should be
  directed to those projects' maintainers.
- Theoretical vulnerabilities without a concrete demonstration.
